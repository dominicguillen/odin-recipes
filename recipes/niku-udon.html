Niku Udon

A Japanese noodle dish consisting of udon noodles, sweet and salty beef, onion, ginger, and green onion, in a dashi broth.

This is a classic Japanese comfort food. It is sweet, salty, and packed full of umami flavor. A perfect week-night dish that can be ready in about 30 minutes.

Ingredients:

Seasoned Beef:

  • 1/2 lb. beef sliced thinly
  • 1/2 medium yellow onion (or 1 small yellow onion)
  • 1" ginger
  • 1 tbsp oil (canola, peanut, avocado, vegetable, ect.)
  • 1 tbsp dark brown sugar (or brown sugar)
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp mirin (sweet rice wine for cooking)
  • 2 tbsp sake
  • 2 tbsp water

Broth:

  • 2 packages of udon noodles
  • 4 cups water
  • 2 tsp dashi powder
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 3 tbsp mirin
  • 2 tbsp sake

Garnish:

  • 4 green onions
  • Schichimi powder to taste (if desired)
  • Kamaboko slices (if desired)

Instructions:

  1. In a pot; add 3-4 cups of water, cover, and place over medium-high heat.
  2. In a second pot; add 4 cups of water, 2 tsp dashi powder, 2 tbsps soy sauce, 3 tbsps mirin, 2 tbsps of sake, and place over medium-high heat. Once boiling, reduce heat and keep broth warm.
  3. Wash and thinly cut green onions. Set aside for garnish.
  4. Peel and cut onion into thin slices (cut perpendicular to the top and root, approximately 5mm thick).
  5. Peel and julienne cut ginger root (approximately 1mm matchsticks).
  6. Combine 1 tbsp dark brown sugar, 3 tbsps soy sauce, 2 tbsps mirin, 2 tbsps sake, 2 tbsps of water, and set aside for seasoning the beef.
  7. In a pan; add oil and place over medium-high heat.
  8. Once oil is heated, add onion and ginger to pan. Cook until onions are slightly browned and translucent.
  9. Add beef to pan and cook until browned.
  10. Once browned, add seasoning and simmer for 15 minutes. Set aside for serving.
  11. While beef simmers, and once water has started to boil, add udon. Cook to package instructions.
  12. Once noodles are cooked, strain into colander and rinse under cold water.
  13. To serve; place noodles in a bowl, ladel broth over noodles, add beef to bowl, garnish with green onions and schichimi powder (if desired).
