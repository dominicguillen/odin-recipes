Butter Chicken (Instant Pot)

A take on a very popular Northern Indian curry dish with chicken.

Best served with fragrant basmati rice and naan, this dish has bites of tender dark-meat chicken in a luscious cream, butter, and tomato based gravy.

Ingredients:

Chicken:

  • 1.5 lb. boneless-skinless chicken thighs
  • 1 tbsp turmeric
  • 1 tbsp cumin
  • 1 tbsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tbsp garam masala
  • 1 tbsp kosher salt
  • 0.5 tbsp cayenne pepper
  • 6-8 cloves of garlic
  • 1 small onion (yellow or red)
  • 1" ginger root (approximately a knob the size of the tip of your thumb)
  • 1 14oz. can of diced tomato
  • 1 handful of roasted cashews (if desired to thicken gravy)
  • 1 tbsp cooking oil

Garnish:

  • 0.5 cup of heavy whipping cream
  • 0.5 cup of fresh cilantro
  • 1 stick of butter (4oz.)
  • 1 tbsp garam masala

Accoutrements:

  • 2 cups uncooked basmati rice
  • 2-4 naan flatbreads

Instructions:

  1. Add 2 cups of uncooked basmati rice and water to a rice cooker, and turn on. Once done, fluff lightly and keep warm until ready to serve.
  2. Peel and roughly chop onion. Crush and peel garlic cloves. Wash and cut ginger root into coins.
  3. Set Instant Pot to saute on high, and add oil. (If chicken thighs are frozen, skip this step, step 4, step 5, and step 6.)
  4. Once oil is heated, add chicken thighs and brown on both sides. Remove chicken and set aside.
  5. Add onion and ginger, cooking until browned and onion has become translucent.
  6. Add garlic and cook until fragrant (about 30-45 seconds).
  7. Turn off the saute function, add tomatoes and spices, and mix thoroughly. (Also add onion, ginger, and garlic if step 4-6 were skipped. Further, add cashews if desired).
  8. Return chicken and place lid on the Instant Pot, pressure cook on high for 15 minutes. (20 minutes if using frozen chicken thighs.)
  9. While chicken is cooking, wash and chop cilantro.
  10. Preheat oven to 400 degrees, and place naan on a baking sheet.
  11. After the Instant Pot timer has gone off, allow to naturally release pressure.
  12. Remove chicken from pot and allow to cool slightly. Chop chicken into 2" cubes.
  13. Add second tbsp of garam masala to remaining liquid, and blend well with a hand blender.
  14. Add heavy whipping cream and cubed butter, stirring constantly until butter has completely melted. (Alternatively; the hand blender can be used, but beware blending cold cubes of butter as this can create a huge mess!)
  15. Lighly spray naan with cold water and bake in the oven for 3-4 minutes.
  16. Return chicken to gravy and add cilantro, reserving a small amount of cilantro for garnishing. Keep warm until ready to serve.
  17. Serve by placing rice and butter chicken in a bowl garnished on top with remaining cilantro, and a naan on the side.
